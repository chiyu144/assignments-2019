What Android App Bundles mean for you

The Android App Bundle is Android's new app publishing format. It means a smaller app for your users and a streamlined, more efficient release process for you. The app bundle also introduces modularization so you can deliver dynamic features on demand and increase discovery with Google Play Instant.

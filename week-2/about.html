What is Dummy Book Cover ?

It's a movement original from Japanese twitter users who post their illustrations and photos like if they were a book cover. The formal name of this movement is called "それっぽくなる表紙". If you don't know Japanese, it's ok as most lines are self-explanatory like "It is a title" or "Additional meaningless text".
